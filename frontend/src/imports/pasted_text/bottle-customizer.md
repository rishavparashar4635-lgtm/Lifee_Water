Add a **“Customize Your Bottle” feature with ultra-realistic 3D visuals** to a premium bottled water website.

The entire experience must feel like interacting with a **real physical bottle**, not animation, illustration, or cartoon-style graphics.

---

# 🎨 DESIGN DIRECTION (STRICT)

* ONLY use **photorealistic 3D renders**
* No cartoon, no flat illustrations, no fake 3D
* Visual quality should match:

  * Product advertisement level (like premium FMCG brands)
  * High-end CGI renders (Blender / Cinema 4D quality)

---

# 🧊 SECTION TITLE

Headline:
“Craft Your Signature Bottle”

Subtext:
“Personalize premium water bottles with precision-designed labels for your special moments.”

---

# 🎯 STEP 1: SELECT OCCASION

### Layout:

* Premium horizontal cards with **realistic 3D product mockups**
* Each card shows an actual bottle with customized label

---

### Occasion Options (REALISTIC VISUALS):

1. Wedding

   * Real 3D bottle with elegant label
   * Label shows bride & groom photo + names + date
   * Soft gold/white luxury theme

2. Corporate

   * Real 3D bottle with company logo printed
   * Clean minimal label (white/blue corporate style)

3. Birthday

   * Real 3D bottle with birthday person image
   * Vibrant but still realistic print quality

---

### Interaction:

* Hover → subtle light reflection shift (not cartoon effect)
* Click → smooth transition to customization

---

# 🎨 STEP 2: LIVE 3D CUSTOMIZATION

### Layout:

Split screen

LEFT:

* **Hyper-realistic 3D bottle model**
* Transparent plastic + water refraction visible
* Label wrapped accurately around bottle
* Bottle rotates slowly (realistic lighting, not flashy animation)

RIGHT:
Customization panel

---

### Customization Controls:

1. Upload Image:

   * High resolution photo (user upload)

2. Text Inputs:

   * Name(s)
   * Event Date
   * Custom message

3. Label Style:

   * Wedding: Elegant gold/white
   * Corporate: Minimal clean
   * Birthday: Soft vibrant tones

4. Label Finish Options:

   * Matte
   * Glossy

---

### REAL-TIME PREVIEW (IMPORTANT)

* Changes must reflect instantly on the 3D bottle
* Label should wrap naturally (no flat overlay)
* Lighting should adjust dynamically

---

CTA:

# ✨ MICRO INTERACTIONS (REALISTIC ONLY)

* Bottle rotation: slow, smooth, natural
* Light reflections shift with movement
* No exaggerated animations
* Smooth UI transitions only

---

# 📱 MOBILE EXPERIENCE

* 3D bottle optimized for performance
* Touch drag to rotate bottle manually
* Simplified controls

---

# 🧠 UX PRINCIPLES

* Focus on **real product feel**
* Make user feel like they are holding the bottle
* Keep UI minimal, product-focused

---

# 🔧 TECH DIRECTION (FOR DESIGNERS & DEVELOPERS)

* 3D assets should be created using:

  * Blender / Cinema 4D (for rendering)
  * Export for web (GLB / USDZ)
* Realistic materials:

  * Transparent plastic
  * Water refraction
  * Label texture

---

# ❌ STRICTLY AVOID

* Cartoon graphics
* Flat mockups
* Fake 3D effects
* Over-glow or neon overload
* Gaming-style visuals

---

# 🔥 FINAL GOAL

Create a **luxury product customization experience** that feels:

* Real
* Premium
* Tangible
* Brand-elevating

The user should feel like they are designing a **real bottle in their hands**, not interacting with a UI.
